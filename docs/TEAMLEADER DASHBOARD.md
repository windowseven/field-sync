❗ WHAT TEAM LEADER CANNOT DO

Team leader does NOT:

❌ Create project
❌ Invite users
❌ Assign roles globally
❌ Create zones (unless allowed by supervisor)
❌ Access other teams (by default)
❌ Change system settings

👉 That belongs to:

Supervisor (project level)
Admin (system level)
