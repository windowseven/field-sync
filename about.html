01 — The Origin

A Mission in ManyaraSparked Everything.

"What if field operations could be managed in real-time, with full visibility, structured data, and smarter coordination?"

In March 2026, our founder participated in an evangelistic mission in Kaloleni, Manyara, Tanzania. Teams moved house-to-house, paper forms in hand, logging visits and responses. At first, it seemed organized.

But as the mission progressed, cracks appeared. Some teams unknowingly revisited the same areas multiple times. Other zones were never touched. There was no visibility, no coordination, no way to measure impact in real-time.

That lived experience — in the red soil of Manyara — became the seed of FieldSync.

02 — The Problem Space

The field challenges are universal, not unique.

001
🗺️

No Spatial Visibility

Teams operate blind — no shared map, no coverage tracking, no live awareness of who's where or what's been done.

002
🔁

Wasted Duplication

Without coordination, teams revisit the same zones while entire areas remain untouched — effort wasted, results diluted.

003
📋

Manual Data Collection

Paper forms are slow, unreliable, and hard to verify. Data arrives too late to be actionable during operations.

004
📊

Weak Analytics

No metrics, no dashboards, no way to measure impact or report to stakeholders with confidence or speed.

005
🔗

Broken Coordination

Leaders issue instructions by call or message, losing context fast. Field decisions happen in isolation without guidance.

006
📡

Zero Real-Time Control

By the time problems are noticed, it's too late. Management has no live view of what's happening across their operation.

05 — The Vision

Built to solve both
sides of field work.

⚡

Execution in the Field

Every team member knows exactly where to go, what to do, and how to capture data. No guesswork. No duplicate effort. Pure, structured execution.

  • Map-based task assignment
  • Digital forms with validation
  • Offline-capable data sync
  • Real-time location sharing

Control at the Platform

Supervisors and admins see everything — live. They manage projects, teams, and outcomes from a single, unified command layer.

  • Live analytics & dashboards

  • Real-time progress tracking
  • Exportable reporting engine

Designed for any field-based operation — not just missions. FieldSync serves every sector that deploys teams into the real world.

  • Census & survey fieldwork

  • Inspection & monitoring

Scalable by Design

From a single mission with 10 volunteers to a national census with thousands of workers — FieldSync scales without losing control or visibility.
